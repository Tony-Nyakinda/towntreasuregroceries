The Journey of Town Treasure

Town Treasure Groceries was born from a simple idea: to make fresh, wholesome food accessible to every home in Nairobi. Our founders, passionate about healthy living and community support, noticed a gap in the market for a reliable, quality-driven grocery delivery service that truly cared about its produce and its people.

Starting small, we built relationships directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers, ensuring that every fruit, vegetable, and pantry staple met our high standards. We believe in transparency and sustainability, bringing you food that's not only good for you but also good for the planet and our local economy.

From humble beginnings, we've grown, but our core values remain unchanged. We continue to innovate, striving to provide a seamless shopping experience, from click to doorstep, making healthy living convenient and enjoyable for all.

Our Mission

At Town Treasure Groceries, our mission is simple yet profound: to connect our vibrant community with the freshest, highest-quality produce and essential pantry items, delivered with unparalleled convenience and care. We firmly believe that nutritious and delicious eating should be an effortless part of everyone's daily life, not a luxury.

We are deeply committed to fostering strong relationships with local farmers and embracing sustainable practices. This dedication ensures that every single item you receive from us is not only bursting with freshness but also ethically sourced, supporting both your well-being and the vitality of our local agricultural landscape.

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Freshness

We pledge to deliver only the freshest, seasonal ingredients. Our rigorous selection process ensures peak quality and taste for your table.

Community Empowerment

By collaborating closely with local farmers and businesses, we strengthen our community's economy and promote sustainable agriculture.

Customer-Centric Experience

Your satisfaction drives us. We're committed to providing an intuitive ordering process, reliable delivery, and responsive support.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a future where every household can effortlessly access fresh, healthy, and sustainably sourced groceries. Town Treasure aims to be the leading name in convenient and conscious grocery delivery, expanding our reach while deepening our commitment to local producers and environmental stewardship. We strive to build a healthier, happier community, one delivery at a time.
